Automate the Register

We are creating this website to solve the major problem of manual tracking and capturing working activities of farmers and also to keep the record of their attendance.
Manual attendance is a bit burdensome due to a wide number of people.

Our main aim is to build a system which can mark attendance of many people at a time or within a single capture to spot.
